ABOUT US

We are a group of people with decades of experience in growing and selling mangos. We know what a good mango looks like and how they are grown. This essential fruit requires a temperate climate, loamy soil that is rich in organic matter apart from needing proper drainage and aeration facilities. Lucky for us we live in Uttar Pradesh which is proudly known as paradise of earth is also home to temperate fruits like mango for which the state is very famous across globe. India contributes to 50% of the total world’s mango production. Uttar Pradesh is India’s largest Mango producing state, producing 4 million tonnes of Mango every year. Mango is a nutritious food and has huge health benefits. Mango is also preferred in various food processing industries because of its nutritional value. Mango is a rich source of Vitamin A and Vitamin C. A good mango variety contains 20% of total soluble sugars. Mango fruit has an acidic level ranging from 0.2 to 0.5% and a protein content of about 1%.

VARIETIES

Alphonso

Alphonso has a delicate taste, aroma, and strong flavour. It is produced in the Ratnagiri region of Maharashtra.

Totapuri

Totapuri is a commercial variety of Mango grown in south India. This fruit size is medium to large and has a mid-season variety.

Kesar

After Alphonso, Kesar is the most expensive variety. Kesar mangoes are sought after as an ingredient for exotic dishes..

Raspuri

Raspuri mangoes are mostly produced in the Mysore district. raspuri mango can be as sweet as the Alphonso mango.

Dashehari

The fruit is small in shape and yellow in colour. The fruit quality is outstanding, and the keeping quality is satisfactory.

Himsagar

Himsagar mangoes are a popular variety of mangoe. The flavour of the Himsagar mango is known for its rich, sweet taste.
